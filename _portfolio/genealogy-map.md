Genealogy Map is a browser-based tool for genealogy enthusiasts: upload a GEDCOM file exported from Ancestry, FamilySearch, MyHeritage, or similar, and instantly see your ancestors plotted on an interactive map. Place names are geocoded server-side, but your family tree data never leaves your browser.
